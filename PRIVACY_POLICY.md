## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Local Storage

- **Analysis history** is stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage
- Local data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it
- You can delete local history at any time within the App

### 5.2 Cloud Storage

- Account data is stored securely in Firebase
- Credit balances are managed through RevenueCat
- Idea submissions are processed by our secure backend API

### 5.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Firebase JWT token authentication for API requests
- SHA256 hashing of device identifiers
- Secure cloud infrastructure

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Account data**: Retained until you delete your account
- **Purchase history**: Retained by RevenueCat per their data retention policy
- **Idea submissions**: Processed in real-time; not permanently stored on our servers after analysis is complete
